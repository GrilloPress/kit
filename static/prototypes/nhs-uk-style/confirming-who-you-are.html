Confirming who you are

To set your data sharing preferences we need to check who you are to stop someone pretending to be you

You will be asked to conmplete a few short steps, after which you will receive a confirmation email or text message.

Before you can start you will need:

  • An email address or mobile number registered with your current GP practice
  • Access to your email or mobile phone
  • Your NHS number, if you have one

If you have not registered your email/mobile details with your GP practice it will not be possible for you to continue with this process online.

Other ways to set your preferences

To continue and set your preferences you must agree to the following statement

Your email and mobile number will not be used for any other purpose. It will never be used for marketing.
