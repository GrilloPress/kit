Your data - Our pledge

Your data is important for providing your individual care and for improving health, care and services through research and planning e.g. monitoring safety and developing new treatments.

When using your data for improving health, care and services, we pledge the following:

  • We will always use anonymised data whenever we can
  • We will only use your personally identifiable data to benefit health and care
  • We will use the minimum information where possible e.g. removing your name and using your NHS number instead
  • We will give you a choice over sharing your personally identifiable data whenever we can
  • We will never use your data for marketing or insurance purposes (unless you specifically tell us that you want us to)
  • We will respect your decision if you opt out of your personally identifiable data being shared beyond your individual care
  • We will only share your information where allowed by the law and where there has been independent scrutiny and approval
  • We will make it clear on our website what we use your data for, who we share information with and why
  • We will always take every step to keep your data secure
