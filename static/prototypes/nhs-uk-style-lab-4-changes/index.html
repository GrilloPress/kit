Register your choice for sharing patient health information

Use this service to register your preferences for sharing your patient health information. See more information about data sharing.

To continue you will need:

  • access to your email or mobile phone
  • your NHS number
Where can I find my NHS number?

You can find your NHS number on any document sent to you by the NHS. This may include:

  • prescriptions
  • test results
  • hospital referral letters
  • appointment letters
  • your NHS medical card

Ask your GP for help if you can't find your NHS number

If you have not registered your email or mobile number with your GP practice, you cannot continue with this online service. See other ways to set your preferences instead.
